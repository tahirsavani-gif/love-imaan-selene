At your core, you are boundless, timeless, endless energy. It's time to step into your power.

Imaan's philosophy

What if none of it
actually defines you?

Everything you think you are is based on arbitrary factors. The family you were born into, the city you grew up in, and every experience that left a mark. Imaan's work starts with a radical question: what if none of that actually defines you?

Because it doesn't. The only thing that can define you is the choice you make in this moment. Who you are right now. How you show up today.

That is where the work begins.

Clarity on what is actually holding you back Permission to become who you already are A path that is entirely and unapologetically yours
what to expect

Imaan will surprise you with her wisdom, perspective and clarity. Her goal is to remind you of the universe that exists within you. You are in control. The world is your oyster. And sometimes you just need that extra nudge before you light up the world around you.
